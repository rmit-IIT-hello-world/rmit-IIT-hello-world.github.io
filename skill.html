Skills and Jobs

A short-term development project requires efficient team management. Therefore, if the team want to complete the project in 6 months, the team need to set up a Scrum team. My position here is the Product owner.

  • Product Owner (PO).
    • The Scrum team has only one product leader who is responsible for developing the most valuable products within a limited time limit. This is done through management of product to-do items that flow to the team, selection and sorting of these items. The product owner maintains the product to-do list (Product Backlog) and ensures that everyone knows the contents and priorities. The product leader will weigh the scope and schedule to select the best product by selecting what the next team should do and what to postpone.
  • Position 1: Scrum Master (SM) (1 person)
    • Job description:
      • Scrum Master's responsibility is to help the Scrum team to follow their process. Scrum Master must have a good understanding of the Scrum framework and could train others to understand the subtleties of Scrum. Another duty of Scrum Master is to note that the obstacles to team advancement have been eliminated. These obstacles may come from outside the team, such as lack of support from another team, or from within, such as product managers who do not know how to prepare product to-do lists properly.
    • Job skills:
      • Cultivate the self-organizing ability of the team.
      • Help teams execute Scrum processes.
      • Responsible for ensuring that internal and external staff have an adequate understanding of Scrum and ensure that Scrum is properly used.
      • Help everyone improve and make the team more efficient and valuable.
