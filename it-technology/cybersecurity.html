Cybersecurity

What does it do?

Cybersecurity has been an indispensable technology since the birth of the internet when it comes to protecting people. If there is no cybersecurity there will be all kinds of criminal intimidation in the internet world and people will be vulnerable to hackers. However, cybersecurity has developed rapidly in the past two decades. This is not only because of the maturity of Internet technology, but also because of the continuous improvement of Internet facilities. Therefore, its rapid development depends on the fast progression of social science and technology. Today's rapid development of science and technology cannot happen without the leap of Internet technology. With the development of Internet technology, people pay more attention to the development of cybersecurity because people have always been like this, hoping that their environment is safe and reliable. Maintaining cybersecurity in a constantly evolving threat landscape is a challenge for all organizations (Searchsecurity 2021). Therefore, people want to develop cybersecurity technology, and this is also in line with the order of social development.

Cybersecurity is also applied to social media, national protection and property security. Now cybersecurity can not only maintain the normal order of the Internet. It can also accelerate the protection of various industrial chains by the Internet. For example, cybersecurity protects the confidential documents of the state in order to ensure that the security of state property is not threatened by foreign illegal forces. Also, cybersecurity is protecting the personal privacy of every mobile phone user in order to ensure that the user's privacy and property will not be disclosed. Next, cybersecurity also protects the intellectual property rights and intellectual property rights of the masses and companies. Some books and copyrighted film and television resources are protected by cybersecurity. In addition, cybersecurity can deny spyware because spyware is a form of cyber infection which is designed to spy on your computer actions and relay that information back to the cyber-criminal. A great cyber security solution, such as Fortinet’s FortiGate firewall, can prevent this spyware from taking effect and ensure that your employees’ actions remain private and confidential within your workplace (Nouveau Solutions 2016). Moreover, cybersecurity can protect productivity because viruses can slow down personal computers to a crawl, and make working on them practically impossible. This can cause a lot of wasted time for your employees, and can often bring your entire business to a standstill (Nouveau Solutions 2016).

Finally, cybersecurity can also protect the national military defense forces from infringement, prevent spies and hackers from invading the national defense forces, and avoid network collapse. So as to stabilize homeland security and people's security.

In the next three years, our cybersecurity will face great challenges. With the continuous progress of science and technology, people's level of education is increasing and all kinds of sophisticated computer talents are constantly introduced by the state. Therefore, breaking through the network defense line is the only way in the future war. In the future, cybersecurity is the top priority of national defense forces and in today's modern world, destroying a country's cybersecurity can destroy a country's network system. If a country does not have a network system, all the battle commands will be almost paralyzed in this country. Moreover, in the future, every entity merchant also needs the protection of cybersecurity. This is because in the future Internet physical stores, all currencies and membership cards used for payment may be electronic virtual. Cybersecurity is to help these merchants solve the problem of property security. Finally, we can achieve national peace and security, and people can live in the network environment at ease.

I think advanced 5G technology can make it possible. Super high network speed can break through the enemy's network defense at a critical moment. I also think the development of supercomputers can help cybersecurity to another level. It is precisely because of the fast network speed and computer processing speed that encryption and decryption become particularly easy. People's sense of security should come from cybersecurity. In short, I believe that the continuous improvement of science and technology will accelerate the rapid development of t of cybersecurity. I hope it can help people solve the difficulties of the information networking era and provide basic security for people.

How will this affect you?

In my daily life, I always use the convenience brought by the Internet. But in the earliest Internet age. Cybersecurity has not been effectively protected. So many times, I worry that the balance in my online account will become less. I'm afraid of being stolen by hackers. Nowadays, cybersecurity has been greatly improved. It not only makes me have great confidence in the security of online shopping. It also makes me really realize that cybersecurity has become an indispensable part of my daily life. I will choose the Internet for social communication. And I will use the Internet to pay my bills. While carrying out these processes, I never worried that hackers or other things would threaten me. Maybe this is the sense of security brought by cybersecurity. I also use the computer to encrypt some of my files. Although the encryption process is very simple, because it is one key encryption. But the whole encryption algorithm is very difficult to crack. This is the change brought to us by the Internet. My friends and family also realize the convenience brought by the Internet. They usually shop on the Internet. Will also use the cash consumption of the network. And most importantly, they have never worried that their private data will be leaked by the Internet. These are the self-confidence and influence brought by cybersecurity in recent years. Therefore, my family and friends and I are very proud of the current level of cybersecurity. At the same time, we are also looking forward to what the future of cybersecurity will look like? What impact will it have on our lives? These are what we expect. We expect it to be stronger and stronger, and it will protect us better and better. This is precisely the impact of cybersecurity has been deeply rooted in the hearts of the people.
