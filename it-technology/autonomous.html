Autonomous Vehicles

What does it do?

Autonomous vehicles are self-driving vehicles that are controlled by an AI program within the cars. Autonomous vehicles are also known as driverless cars because they do not require a driver in a driverless car.

The current year is 2021. Most advanced autonomous vehicles can reach level 3 of SAE(Society of Automotive Engineers). SAE, standing for the Society of Automotive Engineers, determines the intelligence level and automation capabilities of vehicles, ranking through 0 to 5. Level 0 is No Automation. Level 1 is Driver Assistance. Level 2 is Partial Automation. Level 2+ is Advanced Partial Automation. Level 3 is Conditional Automation. Level 4 is High Automation. Level 5 is Full Automation (Wikipedia 2021). At level 3, cars are not required to take control, but only in specific situations. Conditions can be road types, traffic conditions and weather conditions. Because drivers can focus on other tasks including watching films or reading newspapers this level of automation is considered the initial entry point into autonomous driving. However, drivers still need to take over when the system requests it. For example, autopilots in Tesla allow drivers to relax in a traffic jam or on a highway and the system will control acceleration, steering and braking. Drivers still need to monitor the car’s state and prepare to take over the car. There are a number of sensors in an autonomous car, including anti-lock braking systems, active steering, GPS navigation technology, adaptive cruise control lasers and radars. These components support the current autonomous driving technologies.

In the future, it is likely to achieve level 4 which is high automation and there is no need for the driver at most times, but it might be needing the driver at some point. Autonomous cars in level 4 are not required drivers who need to monitor cars’ status all the time. The system will decide whether the driver needs to take over the car or not. In level 4, drivers may safely go to sleep and watch films. However, self-driving in level 4 is supported only in limited areas such as highways or some cities. Once self-driving cars leave certain areas, the car will stop and request driver’s control. Therefore, autonomous taxis and autonomous tracks would be important applications in level 4 as Taxis and tracks always work in a limited number of areas.

There are a number of technologies to achieve autonomous driving. The most important one is deep learning. Deep learning is one kind of machine learning.

Computer vision

Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence (AI) that enables computers to get meaningful information from digital images, videos and other visual inputs and make recommendations based on that information. In self-driving, computer vision can help cars identify moving objects and make driving decisions.

Deep learning

Deep learning is one kind of machine learning algorithm that uses multiple layers to progressively extract higher-level features from the raw input. The system will use deep learning to train a proper driving model with the huge amount of data generated while driving. The system can get driving decisions based on the training model.

Laser radar

Laser radar is a method for deriving distance by targeting an object with a laser and measuring the time for the reflected light to return to the receiver. Laser radars can detect the distance of surrounding vehicles. For example, Xpeng P5 has 2 powerful lidars to improve the detection of moving objects. The system can handle the input of lidars and calculate the object’s distance, direction, speed, altitude and so on.

What is the likely impact?

The potential impact of autonomous cars includes reducing car accident rates, decreasing the number of professional drivers and reducing the use of petroleum fuels. When there will be a large number of fully autonomous vehicles on the road, the chances of accidents will be decreased strongly because most of the accidents are due to human errors. It will likely make our roads much safer and reduce traffic congestions.

Professional drivers will be most likely to be affected by autonomous vehicles. If cars can be driven without any artificial control, taxi companies and trucking companies will not hire many professional drivers. They may lose their jobs. However, the question is whether the traffic law allows cars that do not require any driver. Only one incident can make the public doubt the safety of autonomous driving. For example, the death of Elaine Herzberg was the first recorded case of a pedestrian fatality involving a Uber self-driving car on the evening of March 18, 2018. Then, Uber stopped all self-driving tests under public pressure and sold its autonomous vehicle research division to a startup named Aurora in 2020.

Most autonomous vehicles are electric vehicles. The promotion of electric vehicles can decrease the usage of fossil fuels and abate pollution. More EV(electric vehicle) charging stations will replace gas stations. Electric vehicles usually can have a higher speed compared to traditional cars.

The traffic ticket due to humans’ mistakes on roads or with vehicles is important revenue for the governments to collect money. As only one parking fine costs more than $70, railway crossing on a red light or when the light is blinking can cost up to $750 and there are heaps of fines. The government will have to come up with new traffic rules and regulations.

How will this affect you?

If autonomous vehicles hit the automobile markets, the things it might change for me can be the cost of daily transport, time and car insurance and roadside assistance. I do not have to wait for public transport every morning and I can call a cheap autonomous taxi without a driver. Moreover, AI technology will greatly reduce the probability of accidents. Autonomous vehicle systems can know everything around the car and avoid danger.

Autonomous vehicles might strongly affect my life. I do not need to get a driver license to use an autonomous vehicle. My mom was a doctor and my father is the only one who works to take care of the family, therefore, on working days it is barely possible to spend enough time with the family. The time we can get together is only on weekends. We always go to a supermarket and spend half of the time in the car. My dad is the only one who has a driver licence so he can barely participate in our talks. If we have an autonomous car, my father can safely join our conversion on the way to the supermarket. Moreover, my father will not have to worry about having to take my family shopping if he is the only one with a driver’s license. Autonomous vehicles will be a proper solution.

If autonomous vehicles can freely drive on the road, it will make me relax when driving for work and I can even sleep or read newspapers in the car. If everyone thinks like me, there may be more cars on the road because driving an autonomous car is extremely easy and causes more traffic jams.
