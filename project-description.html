Hello World Team - RMIT

Project Description

Overview

Topic

The project we decided to implement is a food and recipe sharing web application that organizes a list of recipes based on the ingredients in the user’s kitchen and allows people to share their own recipes to other users. It is expected to solve the problem of not knowing how to use the ingredients at home as well as giving ideas for what to make when you don’t have something specific in mind. We hope to finalize a website with a user-friendly interface that all ages of users can enjoy.

In order to achieve this goal, some basic challenges we may face are as follows: the survey of the target user groups of the website, the specific implementation of the website, and the operation of the website after it goes to be deployed.

Motivation

With the rapid advancement of the information society, more and more people are concerned about their own health problems from all aspects. As an indispensable part of people's survival, diet is closely related to people's health. Eating out may encounter various problems, such as the taste of the food is not exactly the same as expected, high-end restaurants have guaranteed food quality but are expensive, and cheaper restaurants may not be able to ensure the quality of food, so cooking at home has become a trend that could encounter both quality and the cost. Today's society is an information explosion society, people can easily obtain any information they want from the Internet, so the original intention of this project is to give people more channels to share and receive food-related information.

Under the current Covid-19 epidemic situation in most countries, many cities and many areas were blocked, so that people had to order takeout to eat. At present, the price of takeout is generally high, and many people can't afford the price. People are restricted from going out and that greatly affects the quality of their meals. In order to solve this problem, we encourage you to buy food materials online through this project and make a delicious meal with the remaining food materials at home. This greatly saves money and is clean and hygienic. The most important point is that this project can enable even a person who can't cook to make delicious dishes.

Landscape

About some competitors of this website, such as simply recipe, yummy website, the functions of these websites are roughly the same as our product functions, which is reflected in some high-priority and medium-priority parts, such as account system, scoring system, comment system. Our application also has innovative features including ingredients shopping system and cross platform. Users can easily purchase ingredients via Amazon Fresh and learn new recipes on their mobile phone and computer.

Aims

The major aim of the project is to build a stage for people to share recipes and encourage more people cooking by themselves. Under the situation of the pandemic and coronavirus, people will spend more time having meal at home. Those who are not able to cook might have to learn to do it and they can use the website as the guidebook. Individuals might be attracted by those interesting recipes and start cooking. For those who are good at cooking, they can share the recipes to others to help them studying.

To achieve the major aim, we should finish some short term aims step by step. The first step is of course the implementation of the website. A lot of things should be done to finish this step such as the user research, functional design and interface design and so on. The user research is to define the major user groups, to analyse the preferences of different target user groups, and then make reasonable adjustments to the page interface and functional design of the website based on these. After the preparation and the design stage of the website, the group should consider the implementation of it. Our goal is to make the website functionality within three months by following the Agile mode. Agile mode is suitable for short term implementations and it requires the group to communicate more during the implementation which may encourage the group finishing the implementation with quality and efficiency. The second aim for the project is to operate the website reasonably. In the initial stage after the website goes deployment, the aim is to attract more users. My first plan is to pay for those vloggers and famous chefs to use the website and share their recipes, then their original followers would be attracted. After that other people may be gradually attracted. Following the initial beginning of the website, what we should do is to ensuring the activeness of existing users. The plan is to set a seasonally cooking competition that users can upload their own cooking videos. The champion is determined through voting by other users. The prize may not be pure cash but similar to the one-month egg supply of the champion family. At the same time, we must also actively absorb users’ opinions, carefully listen to users’ suggestions, make changes to the unreasonable design of the website and fix possible bugs in a timely manner to make sure the website can go further continually.

I also predicted some things that would cause things to develop differently from the expectation. For example, the website development process is not smooth enough which would cause a delaying of the deployment. In this case, the team should strengthen communication, find the reason for the slow progress and correct it. In addition, the initial number of users may not meet expectations. In this case, we can increase advertising efforts to let more people know about our website.

Stages

We are now on the stage of the preparing and planning for the implementation of the application. Before the implementation, what we should consider is that the website requires a dataset that can store all of the user information and recipe details. The quantity of the data is extremely large so we have to borrow or buy the server to store those data. To build our own product, it is necessary to design a logo for website. Then we should consider the UX design of the website interface. The design should follow the principle of concise and clear appearance and substantial content. In the case of sufficient funds, we can consider hiring a professional designer to design the logo and website. After that, we should do the user research. As mentioned above, the user research is to define the major user groups, to analyse the preferences of different target user groups, and then make reasonable adjustments to the page interface and functional design of the website based on these. In case we are not able to do the user research now, we still carefully considered some feasible specific functions which are shown below.

The first function is an account system.
After creating an account, users can log in, log out, and manage the profile. It would show a profile card where users can modify their personal information. Furthermore, users can have their account access more functions, such as posting recipes, managing recipes and writing a comment for a recipe. In addition, only registered users can save and collect their favourite recipes. This function would be the initial one during the implementation because we need to implement two ports - on the user side and the administrator side for better management of user information. Administrator users have more authority and permissions such as deleting inappropriate posts and comments for ordinary users.

The second function is the virtual kitchen and pantry pages.
Users can go to the profile page to access the pantry function. On the pantry page, the user can input the ingredient’s name and select the ingredient. When the user clicks the search recipes button. The system will return related recipes. Registered users can record what ingredients users have in the real kitchen like ingredients so that the system can recommend a suitable recipe for those ingredients. This feature is mainly designed to solve the problem that users cannot go outside or do not want to go outside to buy ingredients. They are limited to having the remaining ingredients at home and would like to quickly cook, so as to find a suitable recipe through this application. Therefore, it is very convenient for users to find the proper recipes they want.

The third function is step-by-step instructions.
This feature is the core feature. Each recipe will have detailed descriptions of ingredients and cooking methods. Besides, step-by-step instructions with pictures or videos will be applied to help the user to cook. Step-by-step instructions pages should contain three parts which are recipe information, ingredients and instructions. For the first part, this section will show the recipe photo and its author. All the related tags will be shown as well. If users want to see more similar recipes, they can click on the tag. The website will look through the database again and show the recipes containing this tag. The second part is ingredients, all the specific names and amounts will be shown. Besides, this part is designed to be a checkbox that users could check if they have those ingredients. For the last part is instructions, detailed steps will be offered with pictures or videos. Users could use those for references. Now there is a question: how can we get recipes? There are two ways to collect recipes for the application. The first way is to collect recipe information from google, we could add some basic typical recipes into the origin database such as making a chocolate cake and barbecue chicken. Then we will invite existing users to contribute and share their own recipes. If a recipe gets a number of likes, we will give a bonus to the writer and encourage more users to write their original recipes and share their cooking skills to make the website more active.

The fourth is buying ingredients online
This function can help users buy missing ingredients online. The system connects Amazon Fresh which allows customers to purchase ingredients online. And the list of ingredients can also be saved to the album, which is convenient for users to view.

Buy the missing ingredients with one click according to your favourite recipe. Users often feel worried because they can't buy suitable ingredients. For example, I would like to eat Tom yum which is a famous Thai food, but I do not have the required ingredients and do not know where I can get the ingredients I need. Making unfamiliar dishes is a very troublesome thing. I hope this feature will make things easier. Here we also have a question: how do online purchasing ingredients work? We still have two ways to implement that feature. First, as mentioned above, when the website has enough traffic, we will email Amazon Fresh to request cooperation and try to connect with Amazon Fresh to buy ingredients. Secondly, if the application has been successful and we have enough funds, we could hire couriers to deliver fresh ingredients from shops including Coles and so on. Of course, this service is only in Australia. We will also provide a map with locations of ingredients shops through google map API so that users know where to buy related ingredients.

On a recipe page, the user has previously entered that there are ingredients at home, so the system will quickly display what ingredients the user lacks under this recipe. There is a one-click buy button under the lack of ingredients. Then the user needs to fill in the receiving address and receiving time. Pay the order by bank card or PayPal. After the payment is successful, the purchase request will be sent to the supermarket or Amazon, and the delivery clerk will deliver the ingredients to the door.

In addition, the group considered some other small features from our brainstorming. Users can like recipes and give comments to recipes. Our application could be a community that focuses on recipe sharing. Even though it might increase server pressure, allowing users to maintain communication can ensure the site’s activity and user stickiness. Additionally, in some recipes with allergens such as milk and peanut, there will be a warning on the page. This function allows users to know the recipe of health risks such as allergies and so on. Lastly, users can create collections to save a recipe and check saved recipes on the profile page. This function may also cause the server pressure but it can increase the frequency of user visiting the website. Taking further consideration, if technology permits, we can build an artificial intelligence backend to recommend relevant recipes to users according to their visit habits and preferences to increase the attractiveness of the website to users. This one might not be easy to implement but we can work towards this goal. Generally, the whole team made contributions to do the planning of the websites and all members gave their suggestions to gain reasonable and necessary functions.

Dead ends

In the process, we encountered several dead ends, mainly about sources, thought restrictions, advertisements, and controversial situations. As we all know, the time spent in the development process, the money and the quality of the final results cannot be achieved at the same time. During the discussion, some people thought that some time could be sacrificed in order to save money and ensure product quality. Others think that products should be made as soon as possible to ensure that funds are invested in the operation and promotion of the website. Each team member has different abilities, thinking, and working styles, so we also have disputes related to the direction of our application development, and it is very time-consuming to resolve these controversial obstacles.

The detailed project description includes not only the description but the user stories and the Acceptance Quiteria of each function, the priority of each function and the flow chart of the functions to show how did the group design and consider the project and to help the new group understand better about the project which let them can get started faster.

Roles

This project will carry out different contents in different cycles. At the same time, the group will change everyone's role when the end of a cycle because it is difficult for the team to decide what role the group members are suitable for in this project. Only through the role-playing of one cycle can gradually realize the skills the group members are good at. Of course, hoping every team member can find their advantages. If the member does not find strong skills, it doesn't matter. The role of the team can help each other. The group divide the project into four cycles: the first cycle is the project plan, the second cycle is the project design, the third cycle is the project development, and the last cycle is the project implementation.

  • Project Plan
    1. Project Manager:
      • The project manager is responsible for assigning resources, prioritizing and coordinating communication among team members. To sum up, it is to keep the project team focused on the right goals. The project manager should also establish a set of working methods to ensure the integrity and quality of the project artifacts. In this project team, everyone is a project manager, and everyone needs full participation in the project. Project managers need to constantly discuss and exchange ideas and solutions.
    2. Demand analyst:
      • Demand analysts need to carefully study the needs of people in the current market and make suggestions for the project's functions. Put forward suggestions and requirements for future projects. In this project team, everyone is a requirement analyst. Each member will collect the demand writing plan. Clear the main body and content of the project, laying the groundwork for future design, development and implementation.
  • Project Design
    1. UI designer：
      • UI designers lead and coordinate the prototyping and formal design of Web interface by following methods:
        • Get requirements for the Web interface （including usability requirements）.
        • Build Web page prototypes.
        • The web prototype design is tested in group meetings.
        • And the appropriate feedback on the Web interface implementation plan (created by team members, such as project managers and Development engineers) is needed.
      • In this cycle, every member will participate in the UI design work, whether prototype design or implementation plan should be the result of collective discussion and democratic deliberation.
  • Project Development
    1. Web Developer:
      • This is the most important role to write code for Web applications running directly on browsers. Developers understand the complexity of the operating system such as macOS and Windows, the development environment, and the framework for writing software on these operating systems. This includes Java, JavaScript, Spring Boot, HTML5/CSS and SQL server. In this cycle, team members should learn as much as possible about programming languages and frameworks, to better participate in the development of projects.
    2. Test Engineer:
      • The test engineer is responsible for executing the test, including setting and executing the test, evaluating the execution process of the test and modifying the error, evaluating the test results and recording the defects found.
  • Project Implementation
    1. Implementation Engineer:
      • Responsible for final product commissioning and deployment.
      • Complete project-related system work.
      • Responsible for the technical support of end-users.
      • Responsible for writing system deployment plan and commissioning editor, maintenance manual, responsible for system implementation plan and plan.
